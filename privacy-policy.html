Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase Abmation Tools, we collect the following information:

  • Name: To personalize your license and communications.
  • Email Address: To deliver your license key and provide support.
  • Phone Number: For payment processing via Razorpay.
  • Device Information: Hardware ID and PC name for license activation (machine-bound licensing).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your license key.
  • To validate your license on activation.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To prevent unauthorized use of the software.

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through Razorpay, a PCI DSS compliant payment gateway. We do not store your credit/debit card details or bank information. Razorpay handles all payment data securely.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your license information is stored securely on Supabase (cloud database). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and secure API access.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Razorpay: For payment processing only.
  • Resend: For transactional email delivery (license key emails).

6. License Validation

Abmation Tools periodically connects to our server to validate your license. This transmits:

  • Your license key
  • Hardware identifier (for machine-binding)
  • PC name

This data is used solely for license verification and anti-piracy protection.

7. Data Retention

We retain your license and purchase data for as long as your license is active, plus an additional 12 months after expiry for record-keeping purposes.
